Nainital with Queen of Hills

6 Nights - 7 Days

Inclusions

  • Accommodation

  • Transfer

  • Meals

  • Sightseeing

Covering beautiful destinations of Uttarakhand such as Nainital, Corbett and Mussoorie, Bhimtal, Khurpa Tal and Sattal, holiday spent at these destinations will be memorable for anyone. It is a break from the maddening crowd and pollution of city life. This trip will be a fabulous page of your dream book. So without thinking much, just book this package and get ready to experience the best days of your life.

Itinerary

Day 1 ✦

Delhi - Nainital (7 hours drive)

  • Arrival Transfer
  • Sightseeing tour
  • Lunch
  • Dinner
Other Benifits (On Arrival)

  • Breakfast

  • Transfer

  • Stay Included

Start the trip by road from Delhi to Nainital. After making it to Nainital, which is a charming hill station in Uttarakhand, check into a hotel. Freshen up and go out to scout the scenic places or enjoy boating in Naini Lake. Located at an elevation of 6358ft above sea level, it is a paradise for both young and old alike.

Day 2 ✦

Nainital

After having scrumptious breakfast, we will take you for a sightseeing tour, which includes the most important places. While exploring the places don’t forget to buy exquisite items from the famous shops of the place.

Day 3 ✦

Nainital - Corbett (2 hours drive)

A morning drive to Corbett National Park where you can relish wildlife safari, which takes you deep in the woods and offers you a glimpse of a variety of animal species such as barking deer, chital, Indian grey mongoose, langur, Indian pangolins and Himalayan black bears. It is the perfect place for both nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. Evening will be spent at the luxury hotel where you can take pleasure in an array of recreational facilities.

Day 4 ✦

Corbett

Leave after breakfast for a jeep safari to explore the wild animals in the jungle. In the afternoon you will be taken to Corbett Museum and Garjiya Devi Temple. Further, you will also enjoy bird watching tour at Corbett. Dinner and night stay in the retreat.

Day 5 ✦

Corbett - Mussoorie (6 hrs drive)

Leave after breakfast for Mussoorie. Check into a hotel situated amid the picturesque surroundings. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6 ✦

Mussoorie

After having delicious meal, head off to the famous attractions of Mussoorie like Mall Road, Gun Hill Point and Kempty Falls. Return to hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 7 ✦

Mussoorie - Delhi

Drive back to Delhi after breakfast. We will leave you to the closest airport or railway station for your trip back home.
