Satpura National Park Wildlife Tour

2 Nights - 3 Days

Inclusions

  • Accommodation

  • Transfer

  • Meals

  • Sightseeing

Satpura National Park is a wildlife empyrean that undercover nature's beauty in the best way possible. Positioned in the Hoshangabad district of Madhya Pradesh, the park proffers the visitors an unmatched chance to explore the lush woods and ravines that are perfectly complemented by serenity spread all over. Beautiful landscape and exotic flora and fauna render Satpura a potential to attract every nature enthusiast and wildlife lover. Moreover, the park offers an assortment of activities. Other than jeep, elephant and canoe safari, the park also offers walking safari that proffers a unique experience to the travelers to explore the park on feet.

This 02 Nights and 03 Days itinerary takes the travelers to wildlife and bird-watching tour to Satpura National Park. The journey starts from Bhopal and the traveler has to proceed to Madhai Range of Satpura. Then, the tourist can opt for Canoe Safari, Elephant Safari or Walking Safari in the park. The tour makes the traveler come close to the wilderness in Satpura in a pleasing and gratifying manner.

On arrival at Bhopal railway station/airport, meet our travel guide who will be there for your welcome and thereafter to take you to Madhai range of Satpura, which is about 4-hour drive. On arrival, check in at the hotel/resort as per the chosen option. In the evening, we will go for a boat safari and have a lifetime experience. Thereafter, return back to the hotel/resort for a relaxing overnight stay.

Satpura is one of the most recent parks in Central India. Lies around a river, Satpura is a Tiger Sanctuary that provides a lifetime experience to the visitors. Major highlights of the sanctuary are boat safari, walking safari, and canoe safari,. Also, the Jeep safari is available in the park and is considered excellent for Bird watching, Gaurs (Indian Bison) and Sloth Bears.

Satpura

We will start our day a little early in the morning today and proceed for morning Jeep Safari that will help you in exploring the wilds in woods. Afterwards, return back to the hotel for lunch. After lunch in the afternoon, we will leave for Elephant Safari/ Canoe Safari/ Walking Safari /Jeep Safari as per the schedule. Safari is the best way of exploring each and every corner of Satpura. Enjoy an overnight stay at the hotel/resort in Satpura.
