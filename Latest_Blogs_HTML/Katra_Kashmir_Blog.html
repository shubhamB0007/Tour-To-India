How to Reach Gulmarg?

By AIR

The nearest airport to Gulmarg is situated in Srinagar. From Srinagar, one has to book a cab to reach Gulmarg, which will take around an hour and a half.

BY RAIL

Jammu Tawi, located at a distance of almost 290 kilometers, is the nearest railway station to Gulmarg which is well connected to most of the other major railway stations in India.

BY ROAD

Tourists can reach Gulmarg by road by boarding a flight to Srinagar and then booking a cab to reach Gulmarg via the Srinagar-Tangmarg road, which is almost a 51 kilometers drive.
