Top Things to do in Pahalgam

Pahalgam, known as the Valley of Shepherds, is famous for its breathtaking beauty, sightseeing opportunities & options for adventure activities. There are many things to do in Pahalgam which promise to make your holiday a fun, exciting and thrilling one. Camping is one of the best things to do in Pahalgam, with the top camping sites in Pahalgam being Panchtarni, Lidderwat & Tarsar Lake. How to Reach Pahalgam?

By AIR

Srinagar Airport is the nearest airport to Pahalgam, located almost 95 kilometers away. Taxis and other private vehicles are available from the airport.

BY RAIL

The nearest railway station to Pahalgam is Jammu Tawi, located almost 254 kilometers away, which is connected to all the other major railway stations of India.

BY ROAD

Tourists can reach Pahalgam by boarding a flight to Srinagar & then booking a cab to reach Pahalgam. Make sure to stop at Pampore on the way, which is famous for its apple orchards.
