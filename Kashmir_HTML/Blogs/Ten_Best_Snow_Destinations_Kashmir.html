Srinagar Tourism

Imagine coming across a painting painted with the best colours and touched by perfect strokes, and immediately hoping that it would come alive at that very moment. That is one of the many thoughts that would visit you on a holiday to Srinagar, which is best enjoyed with our Srinagar tourism holiday packages. A family planning a holiday to Srinagar won’t just enjoy sightseeing with our incredible holiday packages, but treasure every second of it. You get to explore the Mughal Gardens, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, with their terraced garden layouts, water cascades, pools and fountains, is sheer pleasure. Our Srinagar sightseeing tour will also include a visit to the famous tourist places in Srinagar like the Hazratbal Shrine and Shankaracharya Temple, which will acquaint you with its cultural richness. The latter is famous both for its location & historical value & the former because it houses a hair of Prophet Muhammad.

A honeymoon tour to Srinagar, with its paradise-like beauty, is how a dreamy romantic getaway should look like. What is more romantic than taking a ride on a shikhara or staying in a houseboat? Just sit back, relax and watch the lovely views of the green mountains surrounding you on all sides, even as the gondola styled shikara graciously moves ahead on the clear waters of the famous Dal Lake. All the incredible experiences that promise to make your vacation in Srinagar a fun-filled and memorable one are included in our tour packages for Srinagar, that are affordable, customizable as per your requirements and which you cannot get anywhere else.

Things to Do on Srinagar Tourism

Srinagar is one of the perfect places for a relaxed holiday. If you are looking for some interesting things to do here, then you must try the following activities:

Shikara Ride in Kashmir

A Shikhara is a traditional Gondola type light rowing boat which is mostly seen on the pristine Dal Lake, apart from other lakes. It is one of the most incredible and relaxing aspects of a holiday in Kashmir, and should be included in your itinerary if you want to experience the surreal beauty of this region to the fullest. Along with the houseboats, it is also considered to be a cultural symbol. But even apart from leisure and tourism purposes, the shikhara is also used by the locals for seaweed harvesting, fishing and transportation. To watch these beautifully decorated boats glide serenely on the lake is a treat to the eyes, and you should definitely not miss out on this experience during your holidays in Jammu and Kashmir.

Skiing in Jammu and Kashmir

Skiing in Kashmir is one of the most exciting winter sports adventure activities that you can try during your holidays in Kashmir, as it gives you the ultimate adrenaline rush and thrill that adventure enthusiasts are after.

Horse Riding in Jammu and Kashmir

Exploring the beautiful and eye-catching countryside on a horseback is a popular activity in Jammu & Kashmir. Horse riding is an ancient way of travelling around the gorgeous valleys and hilly places of Jammu & Kashmir. Today, a large number of tourists including the family vacationers and honeymoon couples utilize this opportunity to relish the unmatched natural beauty of the popular tourist attractions as well as some pretty lesser-known destinations as well.
