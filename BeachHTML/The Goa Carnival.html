MUMBAI GOA BEACHES TOUR

6 Nights - 7 Days

Inclusions

  • Accommodation

  • Transfer

  • Meals

  • Sightseeing

Goa owes its magnetism to its sandy beaches extended along the sun-drenched coastline. The climate is barmy. The perfect blend of sun, sand and sea makes the tourists fell in love with this amazing destination. The warm hospitality of the people and the breathtaking panoramas render a congenial travel experience to the tourists. Without a doubt, Goa is one of the most sought after beach destinations in the world and it is frequented by vacationers from all around the blue planet.

When this tour is mixed with the opulence of the commercial capital of India- Mumbai, the delight becomes more profound and the trip experience becomes never to be forgotten.This trip jumbles the tour of Mumbai with Goa and caters a complete beach tour. The combination of these two sublime tourist destinations is something that an avid vacationer can't afford to miss.

Itinerary

Day 1 ✦

ARRIVE - MUMBAI

  • Arrival Transfer
  • Sightseeing tour
  • Lunch
  • Dinner
Other Benifits (On Arrival)

  • Breakfast

  • Transfer

  • Stay Included

On arrival in Mumbai, our representative will transfer you to your pre-booked hotel. Check in the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2 ✦

MUMBAI - JUHU BEACH

In the morning, we will be proceeding to explore the Mumbai city. We will visit Gateway of India, Juhu beach and Elephanta Caves. Evening is free for shopping or leisure. Enjoy a night stay at the hotel.

Day 3 ✦

MUMBAI – GOA

In the morning, we will transfer you to the domestic airport to catch the flight for Goa, On arrival in Goa, our representative will transfer you to the hotel. Rest of the day is free to relax on the beach sore. Evening is free to enjoy Goan Carnival Theme (Join a traditional carnival, which is a part of rich Portuguese heritage of Goa. Enjoy singing, dancing, tasting good food and wine and have a wonderful evening in Goa. An overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4 ✦

GOA

Today, we will discover the North and South Goa. In the evening, we offer you fishing village. Fishing villages are part of rich cultural heritage of Goa. Created on hotel's ground, Local Goan fishing village forms an ideal backdrop to make your evening more charming and romantic. In the villages, artists showcase their skills while the village dancers sing their local songs. An overnight at the hotel.

Day 5 ✦

GOA

The day is free to relax alongside the Anjuna beach. In the evening, you can enjoy tasting a variety of Sea food Bar-be-cue on the beach. You can take pleasure of dinning under a light of the shining stars in the sky on succulent lobsters and taste various local seafood delicacies that together forms a part of a delightful bar-be-cue set alongside the lovely stretch of the Goan beach. Also, you can cook your own food on skewers over a camp fire. A local musician captures the magic of the moment on his guitar strings. An overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6 ✦

GOA - MUMBAI

Morning is free for leisure in Goa. In the afternoon, we will process to catch the flight for Mumbai. On arrival in Mumbai, check in at the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 7 ✦

MUMBAI

Today, we will transfer you to the airport to catch the flight for onward destination.
