Upon arrival in Delhi, meet or representatives who will transfer you to the hotel. Overnight stay at the Delhi hotel.

Day 2 ‚ú¶

Delhi

Today in the morning, you will go for city sightseeing tour, which includes places like Raj Ghat (the cremation sites of Mahatma Gandhi), Shanti Vana (the cremation sites of Jawaharlal Nehru) and Jama Masjid. Drive past Red Fort and Chandni Chowk. In the afternoon, visit Qutub Minar (built by Qutub-ud-Din Aibek in 1199), Humayun's Tomb, India Gate (War Memorial Arch) and Lakshminarayan Temple (a modern Hindu Temple). While doing the city tour, you will pass by some famous attractions like President's House, Parliament House, Government Secretariat Buildings and Connaught Place.

Day 3 ‚ú¶

Delhi - Agra (203 Kilometers - 4 Hours)

Embark on a drive to Agra. During the journey, enjoy a short stopover at Sikandra Fort. Upon arrival in Agra, you will be transferred to hotel. After a short nap, get ready to visit Agra Fort. Rest of the evening will be free for leisure. Overnight stay at the Agra hotel.

Day 4 ‚ú¶

Agra - Jaipur (233 Kilometers - 5 Hours)

Start your day with a beautiful smile as today you will be beholding the beauty of iconic Taj Mahal. After a brief visit to Taj Mahal, go back to the hotel, and get ready for the drive to Jaipur, which is approximately 240 kilometers from Agra. During your journey, you will enjoy a short stopover at Fatehpur Sikri. Later, continue your drive to Jaipur. Upon arrival in Jaipur, check in the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 5 ‚ú¶

Jaipur

On the fifth day, you will be going for Jaipur sightseeing tour. You will visit the Amber Fort in the morning. In the afternoon, visit City Palace, Ram Niwas Garden, Birla Temple and Jantar Mantar. You will also pass through Hawa Mahal and bazaars. Overnight stay at the Jaipur hotel.

Day 6 ‚ú¶

Jaipur - Delhi (260 Kilometers - 6 Hours)

Check out from the Jaipur hotel and drive to Delhi. During the journey, you will stop for lunch. Overnight stay at the Delhi hotel.

Day 7 ‚ú¶

Delhi - Goa (By Flight)

After breakfast, you will be transferred to the airport to board a flight for Goa. Upon arrival in Goa, you will be transferred to a hotel for overnight stay.

Day 8 ‚ú¶

Goa

After breakfast at the hotel, you're free to explore Goa on your own. Overnight stay at the Goa hotel.

Day 9 ‚ú¶

Goa

Today as well, you will explore Goa on your own. Overnight stay will be provided in the hotel.

Day 10 ‚ú¶

Goa

On the 10th day as well, you will be exploring Goa at your own pace. Overnight stay in the Goa hotel.

Day 11 ‚ú¶

Goa - Delhi (By Flight)

Post breakfast at the Goa hotel, board a flight for Delhi. Overnight stay at the Delhi hotel.
